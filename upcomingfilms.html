Movie of the Week

This week's movie is "The Shawshank Redemption"

An uplifting tale about hope and friendship, "The Shawshank Redemption" is a must-see for any movie fan. It stars Tim Robbins and Morgan Freeman as prisoners who bond over their time in prison and work together to create a better life for themselves.

Avatar Way of the water

Avatar: The Way of Water is an embodiment of the power of film to transport us to entirely new worlds, transcendent and astonishing in its ambition and its ability to make us believe. Cameron has accomplished what many deemed impossible.

Puss in Boots: The Last wish

Puss in Boots kind of surprised me. Far more witty then I expected it to be, nicely animated and featuring a charming story of forgiveness and redemption that belies its Shrek inspired fractured fairy tale roots, this movie more than won me over..
